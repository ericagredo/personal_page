Qu Parking

    University of Florida has a huge parking issue. It is quite difficult to find parking spaces around campus, so a lot of students resort to city or resident parking where possible. It gets even worse for UF football games. This is when we noticed a lot of students attempting to buy/sell parking spaces. Thus, Qu Parking was born. A team of four worked on the business, where me and a friend focused solely on iOS development. We created a marketplace for users to host their empty spots for extra income and for those in need to rent them. We had around 60 users with a couple of transactions. The above image was Version 3 in the works, but here is the final product (Version 2) in the app store.
